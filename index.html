Every issue of the matchday programme, we hear exclusively from significant figures at the club on our Official Voice pages. In the last issue we featured first team coach Steve Round, who joined the club when Mikel Arteta became manager in December 2019. It was a very proud moment for me to join Arsenal in December 2019, to work at this great football club as part of Mikel’s coaching staff.

My role as part of the coaching staff is first and foremost to assist the manager in coaching the team and to help coach positional units and individuals within the squad.
